Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 12, 2026

Cloud Motion Lab ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Stud Finder Pro mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our App.

Information We Collect

Stud Finder Pro is designed with your privacy in mind. We collect minimal data:

  • Magnetometer Sensor Data: The App accesses your device's magnetometer to detect metal objects. This data is processed entirely on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.
  • Subscription Information: If you purchase a subscription, payment is processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect or store your payment information.

Advertising

The free version of the App displays ads provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve ads. We do not request tracking permission or collect personal data for ad targeting. Ads are served in a non-personalized manner.

For more information about Google's data practices, visit: Google Privacy Policy.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email, or personal information
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not collect or store sensor readings
  • We do not use analytics or tracking SDKs
  • We do not share any data with third parties (except ad serving as noted above)

On-Device Processing

All magnetometer data processing, calibration, and stud detection happens entirely on your device. No sensor data is transmitted over the internet. The App works fully offline.

Children's Privacy

Our App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
